Included on the Free Level

You can get fully set up

Your Free Level access is designed to help you organize your legal foundation and prepare for meaningful support.

  • Build your profile Store company details and ownership information.
  • Complete the Legal Survey Identify gaps and priorities for your consultant to review.
  • Upload legal documents Keep contracts, policies, and formation docs organized in one place.
  • 1 Meeting Credit included Use your credit to meet your Principal Consultant.
  • Request new matters You can submit a new matter request, but attorney review and work require legal credits
What Paid Subscriptions Add

Ongoing support through credits

Paid subscriptions are built for businesses that want legal work completed proactively and continuously.

  • Billable hour credits Credits allow attorneys to complete work within your subscription.
  • Discounted legal services Lower effective rates compared to standard hourly billing.
  • 15-minute check-ins (complimentary) Short check-ins can be booked without using credits.
  • Additional meetings as needed Longer meetings and follow-ups are scheduled using credits.
  • New matters require credits When work begins, credits are applied based on logged attorney time.
Important: Billable legal work requires credits. Credits are only used when attorney time is logged.
